Exhibit Introduction


Museum Guided Tour
The most impressive part.



In this course, what impressed me the most was the production of the slate house. There are very few opportunities to touch the slate house model implementation, although the stone slab keeps falling and the roof slips because it does not stick well, the whole process is really interesting!







The most interesting part



In addition to what I learned in this course, the exhibit I am most interested in is "虹橋" located in the Hall of Human Culture. There is a wooden arch bridge in the museum, which is a replica of the rainbow bridge depicted in the famous Chinese painting "清明上河圖". If I am interested in Chinese culture, I naturally can't miss this exhibit!
