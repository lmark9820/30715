Elephant Introduction



Mammoths and Penghe Ancient Elephants


Its tusks are different from a mammoth's. The Mammoth's tusks are curly, and the Penghe Ancient Elephants’ tusks are long and straight.


Active


They were active during the ice age. To this day, humans are trying to reproduce them.









Penghe Ancient Elephants and Asian Elephants


The Penghe Ancient Elephants and Asian Elephants are blood relatives. The Asian Elephants’ ears are smaller than other elephants'.


Habitats And Size


Their habitats are also different. Mammoth’s habitat is in Tundra. But the Asian Elephant’s habitat is in the jungle.

The other difference is their size. Mammoths are larger than Asian elephants. Mammoths are 8000 kg. And Asian Elephants are 5500 kg.
