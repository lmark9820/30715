Formosan Black Bear Introduction



Nicknamed


Dod bear/ Moon bear


Distributions


Mainly along the remote Central and Snow mountain range.

Mostly appear in the mountainous forests at an elevation of 1000-3000 meters.









Behavior


(1) Move to lower elevations to find food instead of hibernating in winer.

(2) Mainly active during the day: more active in summer and autumn.

(3) Skilled at swimming and climbing.

(4) Easily outrun humans.

(5) Do not keep a fixed den.

(6) Solitary except during the mating or breeding season.


Classified


Classified as Carnivora-Omnivore.








Uniqueness


(1) The largest land animal in Taiwan.

(2) Taiwan's only endemic bear.

(3) The most representative wildlife of Taiwan.

(4) They don't hibernate.

(5) The only bears on the planet that make temporary nests.


Reproduction


(1) Pregnancy period: 6-7 months

(2) Litter size: 1-3 cubs
